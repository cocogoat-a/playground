# Upwork Pre-Call Message Templates

Use these as starting points. Keep the structure, then swap in the most relevant observation, proof point, and CTA.

## 1. Same-Day Reply After Client Response

### Warm lead with direct call CTA

```text
Hi [FirstName],

Thanks for the message.

From a quick look, [specific observation].

I would probably start with [first thing to check or fix], because [reason].

If helpful, we can review it quickly on Upwork video. I can do [Time 1] or [Time 2] Kyiv time. Would either work?
```

### Exploratory lead with interest CTA

```text
Hi [FirstName],

Thanks for reaching out.

I had a quick look and one thing that stands out is [specific observation].

That usually affects [traffic / lead quality / conversion tracking / cost efficiency].

If you want, I can send 3 quick findings first and then we can see if a short call makes sense.
```

## 2. Follow-Up #1 - 24 to 48 Hours

### Tier A

```text
Hi [FirstName],

I took another quick look and noted 3 things that may be holding this back:

1. [Finding 1]
2. [Finding 2]
3. [Finding 3]

This is also close to what we fixed for [case or niche], where [short proof point].

If useful, we can go through the priorities on a short Upwork call. I can do [Time 1] or [Time 2].
```

### Tier B

```text
Hi [FirstName],

One thing I noticed is [specific observation].

It reminds me of a similar case where [short proof point].

If you want, I can outline the first 2-3 things I would check next, or we can review them on a short Upwork call.
```

### Tier C

```text
Hi [FirstName],

Quick note from my side - [specific observation].

That may be worth checking before making bigger changes.

If helpful, I can tell you what I would look at first.
```

## 3. Follow-Up #2 - Day 4 or 5

### Tier A with business trigger

```text
Hi [FirstName],

One more thought from my side.

I noticed [LinkedIn / company / hiring / launch signal], so [relevant pressure point] may matter even more right now.

If that is the case, I would focus first on [priority].

If a quick review helps, I can do [Time 1] or [Time 2] Kyiv time in Upwork video.
```

### Tier B with new angle

```text
Hi [FirstName],

Another angle worth checking is [new observation or likely blocker].

If this is already on your radar, I can suggest the fastest way I would validate it before doing a larger rebuild.

Would it help if I send a short priority list?
```

### Tier C final useful nudge before soft close

```text
Hi [FirstName],

One last quick thought - [short observation or question].

If this is still a priority, I can send a short view on what I would check first.
```

## 4. Final Soft-Close - Day 7 or 8

```text
Hi [FirstName],

I will leave it here for now in case timing is not ideal.

If this is still relevant later, feel free to message me here and I can take another look.
```

### Soft-close with honest availability note

```text
Hi [FirstName],

I will leave it here for now in case the timing is off.

If you want to revisit this later, message me here on Upwork and I will be happy to take another look. My schedule fills up quickly some weeks, but I can let you know what is realistic when you are ready.
```

## 6. Proof Insert Snippets

### SEO

```text
We had a similar SEO case where the main lift came from fixing indexation, content targeting, and internal linking before doing anything more expensive.
```

### PPC

```text
I have seen this in Google Ads accounts where tracking and campaign structure were the main problem, not just bidding.
```

### Local

```text
For local campaigns, small fixes in structure, landing pages, and trust signals often move results faster than a full rebuild.
```

## 7. Lines To Avoid

Do not use these:

- "Just checking in"
- "Bumping this up"
- "Wanted to follow up on my previous message"
- "Let me know if you are still interested" without adding value
- any request to move off Upwork before contract
